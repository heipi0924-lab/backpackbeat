About BackpackBeat

We didn't set out to make a backpack. We set out to leave.

The template looked like this: School → Job → Promotion → House → Marriage → Kids → Retire → The End. CiCi made it to step 3. Then she quit.

On her last day, her boss handed her the backpack he'd bought 20 years ago but never had a chance to use. "Don't come back when things get tough. Because it's going to get tough."

That backpack carried her through the hardest 8 months — 2AM on hostel bathroom floors, dreams where she was still in the conference room, the loneliness of being surrounded by people but having nowhere to belong.

Gradually, she reclaimed what she'd lost in those conference rooms: the right to build her own life. Living between certainty and the terrifying freedom of figuring it out on your own — that's where BackpackBeat was born.

"My boss's backpack retired. It's in my closet now. Too old to use, too meaningful to throw away. Now, my backpack doesn't have to fit the mainstream template either."

That old bag has been to 21 countries. It's why CiCi founded BackpackBeat — for everyone escaping the template, or still deciding whether to.

What We Make

BackpackBeat designs travel backpacks for digital nomads and remote workers — people who work from coworking spaces in Medellín, cafés in Oaxaca, and Airbnbs in cities they've never been to before. Every bag is built around one idea: your gear should keep up with your life.

Our Flagship Bag

The 8805 28L Lightweight Business Travel Backpack is what CiCi wishes she'd had from day one — ultralight at 0.8kg, 360° suitcase-style opening, dedicated 16" laptop compartment, and built for the airport, the coworking space, and everywhere in between.

Our Commitment

We donate 1% of revenue to stray animal rescue. Because the animals waiting on the street deserve someone in their corner too.
