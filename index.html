About the Brand

BackpackBeat was founded by CiCi, a former corporate worker turned full-time digital nomad. After years of testing bags across continents, she built the backpack she always wanted — one that fits a laptop, a life, and a one-way ticket. The brand is registered in the United Kingdom and ships to the US, UK, and EU.
